Noah's Ark

Sharing ochre symbolizes each individual's unity with the group and participation in the ritual. These are some of the images used in an exhibition accompanying Spirit installation at Sydney Customs House as "a gateway to information, ideas and inspiration in the heart of the city" Rather than a group of actors chosen to play rolls, these Photos of everyday Australians that came to take part in urban ritual. Diversity of the people is a testament that Australia has a multicultural spirit akin to a human Noah's Ark.
