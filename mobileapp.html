Story Maps

Story Maps is the first GPS map with indigenous, natural and historical information combined in one easy to follow guide. Story Maps links indigenous knowledge with walking tracks (and scenic drives) of Australia, combining this with useful information based on a customised business directory and e-commerce enabled personal journal. Story
Maps allows users to purchase travel and accommodation; as well as plan and document their journey, with user and app generated content marking a personal route to create a unique 'story map' to share with friends and family or keep as personal memento.
