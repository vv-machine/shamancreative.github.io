Basilisk Stare

Psychosexual drama. It was the first short film in 15 years to get a theatrical release in Australia at the DENDY cinemas, where it screened before their features. Exhibited at MoMA NYC Feb 2005, as part of Australia's brightest filmmakers Due to public demand the film was screened again at MoMA's summer program. It was picked up for TV release by the SUNDACE CHANNEL and FRANCE 3: which still screens it. Showcased at the Japan Academy of The Moving Image, at the second Australian-­‐Japan Film Forum. It toured the national and international film festival circuit from Melbourne to Montreal.
